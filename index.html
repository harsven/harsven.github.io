Sven A. Hartmann

My research interests lie in labor and media economics, with a preference for working on projects that use naturally occurring data.

After completing my Ph.D. at Trier University, I continued my research at Trier as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Chair of Personnel Economics and the Institute for Labour Law and Industrial Relations in the European Union (IAAEU). In the academic year 2021/22, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the University of Oxford.
